D.C. Law 25-208. Fiscal Year 2024 Revised Local Budget Adjustment Temporary Act of 2024.

AN ACT

To adjust, on a temporary basis, certain appropriations in the Fiscal Year 2024 Local Budget Act of 2023 to maintain a balanced budget for the fiscal year ending September 30, 2024.

BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this act may be cited as the "Fiscal Year 2024 Revised Local Budget Adjustment Temporary Act of 2024".

Legislative History (LIMS)

Law 25-208, the “Fiscal Year 2024 Revised Local Budget Adjustment Temporary Act of 2024,” was introduced in the Council and assigned Bill No. 25-788 which was retained by the Council. The bill was adopted on first and second readings on June 12, 2024, and July 9, 2024, respectively. After mayoral review, it was assigned Act No. 25-530 on July 26, 2024, and transmitted to Congress for its review. D.C. Law 25-208 became effective Sept. 18, 2024.

§ 34–1506.02. Disconnection of service during a public health emergency prohibited.

*NOTE: This section was created by temporary legislation that will expire on February 4, 2022.*

(a) For the purposes of this section, the term "public health emergency" means a period of time for which the Mayor has declared a public health emergency pursuant to § 7-2304.01.

(b) An electric company shall not disconnect electric service for non-payment of a bill or fees during a public health emergency or for 15 calendar days thereafter.

(May 9, 2000, D.C. Law 13-107, § 106b; as added June 24, 2021, D.C. Law 24-9, § 307(c), 68 DCR 004824.)

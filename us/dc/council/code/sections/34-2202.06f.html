Code of the District of Columbia

§ 34–2202.06f. Composition and organization of the Water Quality Assurance Advisory Panel.

(a) The Panel established under § 34-2202.06e shall be composed of 9 experts who, at a minimum, demonstrate knowledge in at least one of the following professional fields:

(1) Water quality;

(2) Water treatment;

(3) Toxicology;

(4) Public health;

(5) Civil engineering; or

(6) Environmental engineering.

(b) Of the 9 Panel members, one shall be the General Manager or the General Manager’s designee, and one shall be a representative from the Washington Aqueduct.

(c) Panel members shall be appointed by the Mayor in consultation with the General Manager and the Council.

(Apr. 18, 1996, D.C. Law 11-111, § 206f; as added Mar. 19, 2013, D.C. Law 19-240, § 2(b), 59 DCR 14790.)

Effect of Amendments

The 2013 amendment by D.C. Law 19-240 added this section.

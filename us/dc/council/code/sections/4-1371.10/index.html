Code of the District of Columbia

§ 4–1371.10. Immunity from liability for providing information to Committee.

Any health-care provider or any other person or institution providing information to the Committee pursuant to this subchapter shall have immunity from administrative, civil, or criminal liability that might otherwise be incurred or imposed with respect to the disclosure of the information.

(Oct. 3, 2001, D.C. Law 14-28, § 4610, 48 DCR 6981; Nov. 13, 2021, D.C. Law 24-45, § 3073(h), 68 DCR 010163.)

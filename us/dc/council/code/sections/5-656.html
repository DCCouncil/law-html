Code of the District of Columbia

§ 5–656. Applicability.

(a) Except as provided in subsections (b), (c), (d), and (e) of this section, this subchapter shall apply as of October 1, 2016.

(b) Section 5-654 shall apply as of October 1, 2017.

(c) Section 5-652 shall apply as of October 1, 2018.

(d) Section 5-653|(a)|(1)|(B) and (b)(1)(B) shall apply as of October 1, 2023.

(e) Section 5-653|(a)|(1)|(C) and (b)(1)(C) shall apply as of October 1, 2027.

(Sept. 30, 2004, D.C. Law 15-194, § 656; as added May 1, 2013, D.C. Law 19-311, § 2, 60 DCR 3425; Oct. 8, 2016, D.C. Law 21-160, § 3052(d), 63 DCR 10775; Dec. 13, 2017, D.C. Law 22-33, § 7007, 64 DCR 7652; Oct. 30, 2018, D.C. Law 22-168, § 7013, 65 DCR 9388; Sept. 6, 2023, D.C. Law 25-50, § 3002(b), 70 DCR 10366.)

Effect of Amendments

The 2013 amendment by D.C. Law 19-311 added this section.

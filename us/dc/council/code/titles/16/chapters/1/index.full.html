Code of the District of Columbia

Chapter 1. Account.

§ 16–101. Parties.

An action of account shall and may be brought against the executor and administrator of every guardian, bailiff and receiver; and by one joint-tenant and tenant in common, his executors and administrators, against the other, as bailiff for receiving more than comes to his just share or proportion, and against the executor and administrator of such a joint-tenant or tenant in common.

(Dec. 23, 1963, 77 Stat. 537, Pub. L. 88-241, § 1.)

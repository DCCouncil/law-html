Chapter 5A. MPD Overtime Spending Accountability.

§ 5–581. Metropolitan Police Department overtime pay reporting.

*NOTE: This section was created by temporary legislation that will expire on October 13, 2022.*

(a) The Metropolitan Police Department ("MPD") shall provide a written report every 2 pay periods on MPD's overtime pay spending to the Council.

(b) The reports required by subsection (a) of this section shall include the amount spent year-to-date on overtime pay, and a description of the staffing plan and conditions justifying the overtime pay.

(Mar. 2, 2022, D.C. Law 24-81, § 2, 68 DCR 012382.)

Emergency Legislation

For temporary (90 days) creation of this section, see § 2 of Metropolitan Police Department Overtime Spending Accountability Congressional Review Emergency Act of 2022 (D.C. Act 24-325, Feb. 17, 2022, 69 DCR 001440).

For temporary (90 days) creation of this section, see § 2 of Metropolitan Police Department Overtime Spending Accountability Emergency Act of 2021 (D.C. Act 24-187, Oct. 26, 2021, 68 DCR 011339).
